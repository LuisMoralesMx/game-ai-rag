# Elden Ring Strategy Guide & Lore Manual

## The Lands Between and the Elden Ring
The Lands Between is a fantasy realm ruled by Queen Marika the Eternal. The source of the Erdtree's grace is the **Elden Ring**, which has been shattered. The shards, known as **Great Runes**, were claimed by Marika's demigod children, who went to war, resulting in a stalemate called the Shattering.
You play as a **Tarnished**, an exile who has lost the grace of gold, summoned back to the Lands Between to claim the Great Runes, repair the Elden Ring, and become the **Elden Lord**.

## Key Characters and Roles
- **Melina**: A mysterious maiden who offers the Tarnished a accord. She provides the ability to turn runes into strength (level up) and grants the spectral steed, **Torrent**.
- **Queen Marika the Eternal**: The vessel of the Elden Ring and ruler of the Lands Between. She is a god-like figure who shattered the Elden Ring.
- **Radagon of the Golden Order**: A champion who became Marika's second husband and is, in secret, the male half of Queen Marika's physical body.

## Demi-Gods and Great Runes
To repair the Elden Ring, the Tarnished must defeat Demigods and claim their Great Runes:
1. **Margit the Fell Omen / Morgott the Omen King**: The protector of Leyndell, the Royal Capital. Morgott's Great Rune increases maximum HP.
2. **Godrick the Grafted**: Ruler of Stormveil Castle, known for grafting limbs of others onto himself. His Great Rune raises all attributes.
3. **Starscourge Radahn**: The legendary general of Caelid who arrested the stars. Defeating him triggers a star crash, opening a path to Nokron, the Eternal City. His Great Rune raises maximum HP, FP, and Stamina.
4. **Malenia, Blade of Miquella**: Resides at the base of the Haligtree. She is an optional boss known for her extreme difficulty and her signature move, the Waterfowl Dance. Her Great Rune heals HP when attacking immediately after taking damage.

## Core Gameplay Mechanics
- **Sites of Grace**: Golden lights where players can rest, level up, manage their spells, and fast travel. resting respawns most enemies.
- **Runes**: Collected from defeated enemies. Runes serve as both experience points for leveling up and currency for purchasing items and upgrades. If you die, you drop your runes at the location of your death. Dying again before retrieving them destroys them permanently.
- **Flask of Wondrous Physick**: A customizable flask. Players can mix two "Crystal Tears" found at Minor Erdtrees to gain unique temporary buffs, such as infinite FP or fire damage negation.
