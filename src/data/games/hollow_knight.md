# Hollow Knight Strategy Guide & Lore Manual

## Introduction to Hallownest
Hallownest is a vast, ruined kingdom of bugs and heroes. Beneath the fading town of Dirtmouth, adventurers explore ancient caverns, battle corrupted creatures, and uncover secrets of the plague known as the Infection.

## Weapons and Upgrades
The main weapon in Hollow Knight is the **Nail**. It is a sword-like weapon crafted by the bugs of Hallownest.
You can upgrade your Nail by visiting the **Nailsmith**, who resides in the far left of the **City of Tears**.

### Nail Upgrade Stages:
1. **Old Nail**: The starting weapon. Deals 5 damage.
2. **Sharpened Nail**: Requires 250 Geo. Deals 9 damage.
3. **Channelled Nail**: Requires 800 Geo and 1 Pale Ore. Deals 13 damage.
4. **Coiled Nail**: Requires 2000 Geo and 2 Pale Ore. Deals 17 damage.
5. **Pure Nail**: The ultimate upgrade. Requires 4000 Geo and 3 Pale Ore. Deals 21 damage.

### Pale Ore Locations
Pale Ore is a rare, gleaming metal used to upgrade the Nail. There are 6 Pale Ores in total:
- **Ancient Basin**: Located at the bottom-right, guarded by two Lesser Mawleks.
- **Crystal Peak**: At the very top of the peak, next to the statue of the Radiance.
- **Resting Grounds**: Awarded by the Seer after gathering 300 Essence.
- **Deepnest**: Guarded by the boss Nosk in his lair.
- **Kingdom's Edge**: Awarded for completing the Trial of the Conqueror (2nd trial) in the Colosseum of Fools.
- **Forgotten Crossroads**: Awarded by the Grubfather after rescuing 31 Grubs.

## Essential Abilities and Spells
Abilities allow you to traverse new areas of Hallownest:
- **Mothwing Cloak (Dash)**: Found in Greenpath after defeating Hornet. Allows you to dash forward.
- **Mantis Claw (Wall Jump)**: Found in the Mantis Village. Allows you to cling to and jump off vertical walls.
- **Monarch Wings (Double Jump)**: Found in the Ancient Basin after defeating the Broken Vessel. Allows you to jump a second time while mid-air.
- **Crystal Heart (Super Dash)**: Found in the Crystal Peak. Allows you to launch yourself forward in a straight line until interrupted.
- **Isma's Tear**: Found in Isma's Grove (Royal Waterways). Grants immunity to acid water, letting you swim in it.

## Key Bosses and Lore
- **Hornet**: The protector of Hallownest's ruins. She tests the Knight's strength in Greenpath and Kingdom's Edge. She is the daughter of the Pale King and Herrah the Beast.
- **The Hollow Knight**: A vessel chosen by the Pale King to seal the Infection. However, due to an instilled idea (a tarnished purity), they became imperfect, allowing the Infection to leak out.
- **The Radiance**: The source of the Infection and the ancient light deity of the moths. To defeat her, the Knight must obtain the Void Heart and use the Dream Nail on the Hollow Knight at the end of the game.
