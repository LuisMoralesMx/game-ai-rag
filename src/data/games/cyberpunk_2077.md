# Cyberpunk 2077 Strategy Guide & Lore Manual

## Welcome to Night City
Night City is a sprawling, dystopian megalopolis in the Free State of Northern California, controlled by corporations and plagued by gang warfare.
Players control **V**, a mercenary outlaw who, after a heist gone wrong, ends up with a prototype biochip in their head. The chip contains the digital construct of legendary rockerboy and terrorist **Johnny Silverhand**, and is slowly overwriting V's mind.

## Core Concepts & Mechanics
- **Cyberware**: Cybernetic implants that can be installed at a **Ripperdoc**. Cyberware improves physical capabilities, adds armor, and enables special combat features like the Sandevistan (slows down time) or Mantis Blades (arm-mounted melee blades).
- **Quickhacks**: Digital programs uploaded into enemies or devices through V's Cyberdeck. They require **RAM** to execute. Examples include *Short Circuit* (deals electrical damage), *Ping* (reveals all network devices), and *System Collapse* (silently knocks out a target).
- **Street Cred**: Reputation currency gained by completing gigs, side jobs, and bounties. Higher Street Cred unlocks new cyberware, weapons, and outfits at vendors across Night City.

## Key Factions & Corporations
- **Arasaka Corporation**: A massive Japanese megacorporation specializing in security, banking, and manufacturing. Led by Saburo Arasaka and later his son Yorinobu. They developed the **Relic** biochip and the "Secure Your Soul" program.
- **Militech**: Arasaka's primary American competitor, specializing in arms manufacturing and military contracting.
- **The Voodoo Boys**: A mysterious Netrunner gang based in Pacifica, obsessed with breaking through the **Blackwall** to contact autonomous rogue AIs.
- **Aldecaldos**: A clan of Nomads operating in the Badlands surrounding Night City, led by Saul and featuring Panam Palmer.

## Key Characters
- **Johnny Silverhand**: Former lead singer of Samurai and military veteran who blew up Arasaka Tower in 2023. Played by Keanu Reeves, he exists as a voice in V's head, sharing his memories and cynical commentary.
- **Panam Palmer**: A former Nomad mercenary who helps V in exchange for assistance reclaiming her clan's tank, the Basilisk. She represents the Nomad path/ending.
- **Judy Alvarez**: A skilled Braindance editor and member of the Mox gang in Kabuki, dedicated to helping her friends and fixing Night City's sex worker industry.
