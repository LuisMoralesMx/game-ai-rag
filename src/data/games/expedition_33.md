# Clair Obscur: Expedition 33 Strategy Guide & Lore Manual

## Lore and the Paintress
Once a year, the **Paintress** wakes and paints a number on her monolith. Everyone of that age turns to dust instantly, erased from existence. 
Last year, she painted "34". Tomorrow, she will paint "**33**". 
To break this cycle, the members of **Expedition 33** set out on a desperate journey to locate and destroy the Paintress before she can paint the number 33. The world is called **Lumière**, and it bears the scars of a catastrophic event known as the **Fracture** from 67 years ago.

## Playable Characters
- **Maelle**: The youngest member of the expedition at age 16 and a central protagonist. She joins because of an intense desire to see the world beyond Lumière.
- **Gustave**: A dedicated engineer and Maelle's foster brother. He manages the group's technology, including the **Lumina Converter**, and fights using a sword, a gun, and a mechanical arm.
- **Sciel**: A former teacher who wields a scythe in battle and utilizes special mechanics like "Foretell" stacks to plan strategic combos.
- **Lune**: A powerful mage who specializes in casting elemental pictos and using Lumina-based attacks.

## Core Gameplay Mechanics
- **Reactive Turn-Based Combat**: A unique battle system blending traditional menu-driven turns with real-time reflex inputs. When enemies attack, you must actively press keys/buttons to **dodge**, **parry**, or **jump** in real-time. A perfect parry negates all damage and opens up opportunities for devastating counters.
- **Battle Wheel and Action Points (AP)**: You select actions using a visual Battle Wheel. Actions consume AP. AP is built by performing successful basic attacks, landing perfect parries, or dodging enemy hits.
- **Lumina Converter & Pictos**: A device maintained by Gustave that channels Lumina (light energy) into powerful combat spells called **Pictos**.
- **Overcharge Gauge**: Gustave's character-specific combat mechanic. Building the gauge increases his mechanical arm's power, unleashing high-damage burst attacks.
- **Campfire Bond Skills**: Unique skills unlocked by resting at camps and developing relationships between members. These "Gradient Skills" can grant extra actions per turn.
